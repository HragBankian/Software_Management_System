What is GetSoft?

GetSoft is your all-in-one solution for seamless software license management. Whether you're a software user looking to organize and keep track of your licenses or a software provider that is aiming the efficient distribution and monitoring of your products, GetSoft has you covered

Why GetSoft?

GetSoft simplifies the complex world of software licenses. We understand the challenges faced by both users and providers in managing licenses. Our platform is designed to make this process intuitive and secure.
